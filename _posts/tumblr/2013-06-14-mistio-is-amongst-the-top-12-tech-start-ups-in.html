We have just returned from Berlin and Tech All Stars 2013 , an event organized by the European Commission to connect the top 12 European startups. Mist.io was selected out of 300 applicants to join the privileged 12 for the first round of the event.

We had an excellent time in these 3 days. We pitched, discussed technical & entrepreneurial issues with the rest of the teams and learned what’s the status in terms of technology and startups in other European countries.

Mike presenting Mist.io

More importantly, we got to connect with really interesting people who have great ideas and great stories like those from Snap Fashion, Babelverse and Infantium, amongst others.

The 12 teams networking and enjoying a great breakfast.

Congratulations to the 3 teams, Snap Fashion, Trustev and Echochain that made it to London for the next round and to Trustev for the first place. Hope we’ll meet again!
